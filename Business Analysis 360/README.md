# 💼 Business Analysis 360: Global Business Performance Dashboard

An **interactive Power BI solution** designed to deliver a **comprehensive business overview**, integrating data across multiple departments to help executives and analysts make informed, strategic decisions based on real-time insights.

---

## 🧠 Project Overview

The **Business Analysis 360 Dashboard** is an analytical Power BI report developed to explore and evaluate the key business functions of **Atlique Hardware** — a global technology company specializing in the sales of **PCs, laptops, and computer components** worldwide.

This dashboard consolidates data from diverse business domains, enabling stakeholders to **monitor profitability**, **optimize supply chain operations**, **analyze marketing performance**, and **enhance sales efficiency** across global markets.

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Primary data visualization platform for dashboard creation.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing datasets.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures, dynamic KPIs, and business logic implementation.
* 🧩 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established among key business tables (Sales, Marketing, Finance, Supply Chain, Executive) to enable cross-filtering and accurate aggregation.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard previews.

## 🧩 Business Problem

*Atlique Hardware*, a multinational computer hardware company, required a **unified reporting solution** to gain comprehensive insights into its business performance across multiple functions — including **Sales**, **Marketing**, **Finance**, and **Supply Chain**.

Previously, fragmented reports and manual analyses limited the company’s ability to identify **profit drivers**, **operational inefficiencies**, and **regional performance trends**.

**Key Business Questions Addressed:**

* Which region, customer, and segment show the highest growth in sales and gross margin?
* Who are the top and bottom customers and products by net sales?
* What is the performance matrix across regions?
* How does forecast accuracy compare with actual sales performance?

These questions were difficult to answer efficiently using traditional static reports or raw data alone.

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

To design an **integrated business intelligence solution** that:

* Consolidates insights from all business units into a single, interactive platform.
* Provides **real-time performance tracking** through key financial and operational KPIs.
* Enhances **decision-making** with data-driven visibility into revenue, costs, profits, and supply chain efficiency.

---

## 📈 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

* **🏠 Home Page:** Interactive navigation hub with buttons linking to detailed functional dashboards.
* **💰 Finance View:** Profit and loss statement visualization, net sales trends, and year-over-year financial performance.
* **📦 Sales View:** Net sales analysis, gross margin, and gross margin percentage by product, region, and segment.
* **🎯 Marketing View:** Marketing expenses, cost of goods sold (COGS), net profit, and net profit percentage.
* **🚚 Supply Chain View:** Forecast accuracy, net error, net error percentage, and risk analysis.
* **💼 Executive View:** High-level strategic insights including key metrics by sub-zone, revenue by division and channel, annual revenue trends, net profit percentage, and market share distribution.

---

## 💡 Business Impact & Insights

* **Executive Decision Support:** Provides a single source of truth for organizational KPIs, enabling real-time strategic monitoring.
* **Financial Clarity:** Tracks profitability, expenses, and growth trends with precision.
* **Sales Optimization:** Highlights top-performing products, customers, and regions for revenue maximization.
* **Marketing Intelligence:** Evaluates campaign efficiency and ROI to inform future marketing strategies.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Enhances supply chain performance by identifying delays, risks, and cost-saving opportunities.
